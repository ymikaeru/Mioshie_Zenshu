　　We offer here some English translations of Mentor Okada Jikan's writings. The translations presented so far are very few, but we are trying to add more articles gradually to the list, and improve them continually. We are very happy if this project helps both you and ourselves together understand the writings, teachings and thoughts of Meishusama.
